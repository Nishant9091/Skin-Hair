PRP

Platelet Rich Plasma, or PRP, has emerged as a revolutionary non-surgical technique in the realm of hair restoration. This cutting-edge treatment involves the injection of platelet-rich plasma, a substance teeming with a supra-physiological concentration of platelets and an abundance of growth factors and bioactive proteins. These components work in tandem to stimulate the inflammatory cascade, expedite the healing process, and accelerate tissue remodeling. PRP therapy, touted for its natural approach, not only ensures safety but also delivers impressive results.
