WHAT IS CLEARSKIN PRO?

With incredibly strong energy emission and a high degree of safety, ClearSkin PRO is an FDA-approved, award-winning laser therapy that has excellent documented outcomes for skin rejuvenation procedures. In terms of therapeutic anti-aging therapies, ClearSkin PRO represents a big evolutionary step forward and can provide remarkable effects for indications including wrinkles, lines, tightening of pores, and lines. Through the use of simultaneous contact cooling technology and a non-ablative laser, the therapy encourages the growth of new tissue while preserving the skin's high degree of comfort.

The skin may seem somewhat pink following the procedure, but this should go away in no more than eight hours. There is no healing time since you may put makeup on your face the very following day.

Let us introduce you to the ClearSkin Pro Laser by Alma.

  • Advanced Acne Management:
    ClearSkin Pro is not just another acne solution; it's a comprehensive approach to acne management. It targets the sebaceous glands to reduce oil production, minimise inflammation, and effectively combat acne at its source.
  • Acne Scar Reduction:
    ClearSkin Pro is a game-changer for post acne scars, it employs fractional laser technology to minimise the appearance of acne scars, smoothing your skin's texture and revealing a fresh, flawless complexion.
  • Youthful Radiance:
    ClearSkin Pro is also used to rejuvenate skin. The treatment stimulates collagen production, resulting in a more youthful, radiant appearance, effectively combating the early signs of ageing and leaving you with revitalised skin.
  • Clinically Proven Results:
    Alma Lasers, a global leader in the aesthetic industry, powers ClearSkin Pro. With a solid foundation of scientific research and clinical success, it delivers safe and effective results, giving you confidence in your journey to a clearer, healthier skin.

HOW DOES CLEARSKIN PRO WORK?

While the skin's surface is unaltered, it permeates the skin's deep layers. In particular, this implies that there is no recuperation time and that treatments may be planned for the entire year.
Deeply penetrating the skin, the ClearSkin Pro Laser has double the power of the previous generation. Skin renewal results from the heat's creation of deep, narrow microchannels and stimulation of fibroblast cells to make collagen and elastin. Additionally, the primary cause of acne, Propionibacterium, is destroyed by heat. The bacterial proliferation clogs the pores and causes a significant build-up of sebum, resulting in the creation of acne and pimples. The cooling system protects the skin and makes the treatment comfortable and safe while the strong suction removes sebum and cleans the pores.
