HAIR

Hair Transplant
Satya specializes in offering every type of natural hair transplant, including FUE, FUT, and MHT. These techniques can be used individually or combined to ensure a full head of hair.
Hairloss Treatments
Hair loss treatment encompasses accurate diagnosis, medication, lifestyle changes, and advanced therapies such as PRP, GFC, mesotherapy, among others, to effectively combat hair thinning.

Satya Aesthetics & Hair Solutions, established in 2005 stands as a global leader in medical dermatology and hair restoration. It’s a premier institute, renowned for its international state-of-the-art facility & specialises in skin lasers, anti-aging treatments, and both surgical and non-surgical hair restorations, with a particular expertise in corrective/repair hair transplant surgeries. Founded with the mission to deliver world-class, cutting-edge anti-aging and hair restoration treatments, Satya is driven by a highly skilled and trained team. Satya’s guiding principles have always been honesty, truth, artistic imagination, expertise, and compassion.

Our Founders:

Dr. Shaiil Gupta

MD, Dermatologist, Hair Transplant Surgeon

Dr. Shaiil Gupta, is a board-certified dermatologist and esteemed hair transplant expert & founder of Satya Aesthetics & Hair Solutions. Dr. Gupta focuses on holistic treatment, addressing both physical and emotional health in his consultations. Beyond his clinic, he educates and advocates for skin and hair care on social media, sharing his knowledge widely. His multifaceted talents as a musician, artist, singer, writer, composer, and sportsman, along with his role as a subconscious healer, allow him to motivate patients and society at large, establishing him as a global authority in dermatology and hair restoration.

Dr. Ruchi Agarwal

MD, Dermatologist, Hair Transplant Surgeon

Dr. Ruchi Agarwal, board-certified dermatologist and co-founder of Satya Aesthetics & Hair Solutions, boasts over 20 years in clinical and cosmetic dermatology. She excels in dermato-surgical and aesthetic procedures, backed by world-class training. Known for her patient-focused approach & listening skills, Dr. Ruchi emphasises personalised treatment plans and compassionately, blending patient needs with expectations.
