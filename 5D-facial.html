What is a 5D Skin Revival Facial?

5D Skin Revival Facial Treatment at Satya Skin & Hair Clinic in Delhi is a truly unique and highly effective medi-facial. It is a combination of the most useful technologies for skin care.

The 5D facial treatment incorporates five steps:
STEP 1. A relaxing massage.
STEP 2. Deep cleansing of the skin and gentle exfoliation of dead cells.
STEP 3. Skin rejuvenation using IPL photofacial technology.
STEP 4. Ultrasonic infusion of anti-aging and cell rejuvenating serums.
STEP 5. Application of a soothing and nourishing mask.

The 5D Skin Revival Facial helps in the holistic care of the skin and gives you a radiant and glowing skin. Everybody will notice the glow of your skin but no one will know your secret as this procedure has absolutely no social downtime & the results are instant. This is one of those skin brightening medical facial treatments which your skin would love.

Why do we need medical 5D facial treatments?

Our skin is the outermost shield which has to deal with all kinds of environmental stressors like the sunlight, extremes of temperature, pollution and humidity. These factors often result in skin issues like abnormal pigmentation, enlarged pores, fine lines, dull lustreless skin, etc. The medical facial treatments like the ‘5D Skin Revival Facial treatment’’ at Satya Skin and Hair Clinic are scientifically crafted to address these problems and would help to regain the lost radiance and youthfulness of your skin.

How frequently should it be done?

A 5D Skin Revival facial treatment is recommended every 4 weeks for the first 3-4 sessions by our skin doctors. This is to give an initial push to hasten the recovery of the damaged skin. Later on, we recommend continuing this medi-facial every 8 weeks to maintain the results.

  • What are the indications of the 5D Skin Revival Facial Treatment in Delhi?

    The 5D Skin Revival facial is a wonderful medi-facial as it addresses a lot of skin issues:

    • Open pores
    • Dull skin
    • Uneven skin tone
    • Pigmentation
    • Redness or increased sensitivity
    • Fine lines
    • Acne
    • Freckles
    • Broken capillaries

  • What is the right age to get a 5D Skin Revival Facial treatment?

    5D Skin Revival facial treatment in Delhi can be done in all adults of any gender. In younger individuals, it helps to control acne breakouts as well as maintain the glow whereas in slightly older age groups, it helps combat the signs of ageing.

  • What is the mechanism of action of 5D Skin Revival facial treatment?

    Satya’s 5D Skin Revival Facial Treatment is a holistic program designed by the best dermatologists in Delhi to reset your skin to a more vibrant self.
    The first step involves a relaxing massage. This helps in relieving the cellular stress and lymphatic drainage of toxins as well as extra fluid from the tissues.
    The second step of 5D facial treatment in Delhi is deep cleansing followed by gentle exfoliation; this helps to unclog your pores as well as remove the top dead layer of skin.
    The third step of 5D facial treatment in Delhi is photo rejuvenation using the Intense Pulse Light laser. It has multiple benefits as follows:

    • It induces new collagen and elastin fibre synthesis in the skin, thus making the skin soft and supple.
    • It improves blood circulation in the skin, thus making the skin glow.
    • The melanin component acts as a chromophore which is destroyed by heat generated by the treatment, thus it helps in reducing the pigment.
    • It reduces acne by acting on the bacteria called P.acnes.

    The fourth step of Satya’s 5D facial treatment in Delhi involves ultrasonic delivery of serums rich in skin vitamins and growth factors directly to the cells in epidermis and dermis. We choose these serums according to the skin issues which you have.
    The fifth step is application of a soothing mask to help your skin to cool down and imbibe the benefits of all the previous steps.
