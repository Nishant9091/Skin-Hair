What is mole?

Moles are a type of skin growth that can be seen commonly in people. They are small, dark brown spots. Clusters of pigmented cells cause moles. Moles usually appear during childhood and adolescence. There can be 10-40 moles on an average and some among them may tend to fade away or become big in size or change in colour.
